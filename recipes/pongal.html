Pongal

Description

Ven pongal is a delicious and savoury dish that is offered to the gods.

It is not only cooked for households, but is also commonplace in temples during auspicious days. Pongal is a comfort food for South Indians. It can be eaten with vada and chutney as sides.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Moong
  • Ghee
  • Cashews
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Rinse both rice and the roasted moong dal a couple of times with water.
  2. Drain very well and then add them in a 3 litre stovetop pressure cooker.
  3. Pressure cook on a medium to high heat for 7 to 8 whistles or 11 to 12 minutes. For moong dal which cooks quickly, you can pressure cook for 2 to 3 whistles.
  4. Let the pressure settle down on its own in the cooker and then only you remove the lid to check the doneness and consistency.

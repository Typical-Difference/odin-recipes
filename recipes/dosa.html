Dosa

Description

Dosa is a traditional dish predominantly popular in South India. Dosa is known by different names across different regions of India.

Dosa is usually made with rice batter but there can also be variations in the batter type. The making of dosa can also vary depending on the type of dosa that one intends to make. This difference may arise due to a diffrent flavored batter or something as simple as substituting ghee for oil.

Ingredients

  • Rice batter
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda(Optional)

Steps

  1. Mix batter properly before pouring onto pan. Ensure pan is sufficiently hot.
  2. Pour the batter onto pan and make a neat circle.
  3. Pour oil as required on top.
  4. Wait for a while and flip the dosa onto the other side. Your dosa is now ready.

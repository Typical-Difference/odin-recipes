Vada

Description

Vada is a popular dish in South India. Vada is mainly craved by people as a snack.

Vada can be made in different ways based on the composition of ingredients. Here is the recipe to make medhu vada, an important variety. Vada is deep fried in oil to make it crispy and tasty.

Steps
